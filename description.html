Ingredients

-Beef
-Shaki (cow tripe)
-Smoked fish
-Dry fish
-Stock fish
-20 cl palm oil
-500g Efo Shoko
-5 tatashe peppers
-2 tablespoons ground crayfish
-2 red onions
-2 small stock cubes
-2 tablespoons locust beans (a.k.a iru)
-Salt to taste
-Scotch Bonnet peppers (a.k.a tatashe)

View Alternative Ingredients
Preparation
1hr 30 minutes 4 portions

Start cooking the shaki first with as little water as possible as it is the toughest meat in the bunch.
-This soup should have as little water as possible so add little amounts of water at a time and top as you cook.

-When the shaki starts to curl, add the dry fish and stockfish.

-When the shaki is almost done, add beef, stock cubes, & some onions

-Cook till all the meat and fish are well done. Set these aside.

-In another pot, pour the palm oil and heat it up. Once heated, add the remaining onions.

-Fry the tatashe peppers till there's no more water in it. This should take about 15 minutes.

-Add the locust beans, crayfish, and stir very well.

-Add the cooked meat and fish, stir very well.

-Add the vegetables, stir very well, cover and once it heats up again, add salt to taste and take the pot off the stove.
